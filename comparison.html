Weather.Next

Weather.Next provides a best-in-class way to get daily weather forecasts on your phone. It's optimized for the experience of getting an mail alert, appearing in notification center, preview in mail and finally viewing the message. We optimize all of that for you.

Ads? We don't need our emails to be desktop formatted with 50% advertisements.
